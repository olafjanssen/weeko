Weeko Calendar Setup

How to use:

  1. Make your Google Calendar public (Settings → Access permissions)
  2. Get your calendar ID (format: xxx@group.calendar.google.com)
  3. Paste it below and click "Save & Load"

Or paste a Google Calendar shareable link and the ID will be extracted automatically.

Optional: Add your own Google API key for development (get one from Google Cloud Console).
